Streamr

The best friend to bring to the movies!
Streamr is an app to enhance online movie experience by adding a social aspect. Streamr let you create timecoded streams to a given episode/movie that you can play on your second screen while watching. In the streams you can find comments, images, observations, links to other pages for reading more, actor bios, mini reviews, audio comments, an basically everything else you want to know about a movie. You can watch everything posted from the start of the movie till the point you have watched.
Ever watched a movie and just wondered where you have seen the actor before? Not if you watch movies with Streamr!
  • Browse popular streams
  • Create your own streams
  • And it can be free!
