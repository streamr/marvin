About

Streamr enhances online movie experiences by adding a social aspect. Streamr lets you create timecoded streams to a given episode/movie that you can play on your second screen while watching. In the streams you can find comments, images, observations, links to other pages for reading more, actor bios, mini reviews, audio comments, and basically everything else you want to know while watching a movie.

Streamr has a strong focus on not disrupting your movie experience, but wants to be there for you when there's something you're wondering about. New actor on screen that you know you've seen somewhere? A glance down on streamr is probably all you need. If however you're in a more social setting and want to be reminded about funny trivias during the movie, enable notifications and you can be center of all laughs during the movie.

Streamr is a strictly second screen experience, playing back content on your phone or tablet while the main movie plays on either your laptop, TV or home cinema. Sick and tired of annotations on YouTube? Streamr never comes between you and your movie.
